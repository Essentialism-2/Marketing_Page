What is Essentialism?

Pick your essentials.

    Choose from a list of goals the mean the most to you. Then, refine your list down to the top three. Once you've choosen your top three goals that mean the most to you, you can move on to organizing your current tasks.

Organize your current goals.

Take your top three goals and your current to-do list, combine them! Organize your to-do list into the categories that you picked; that way you can easily prioritize the tasks that should take priority in your life.

Get things done!

You're right on your way to getting things done that matter to you! With your new prioritized list of tasked, you'll be able to accomplish tasks that mean more to you.
